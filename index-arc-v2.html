Primal Urge Pet Snacks are 100% Freeze-dried chicken its high in protein and low in fat, and can be a good option for pets that need to maintain a healthy weight or are on a restricted diet. Here are some nutritional values for our freeze-dried chicken:

Protein: Freeze-dried chicken can contain at least 35% crude protein.

Fat: Freeze-dried chicken can contain at least 30% crude fat.

Fiber: Freeze-dried chicken can contain a maximum of 2% crude fiber.

Moisture: Freeze-dried chicken can contain a maximum of 6% moisture.

Calories: A 0.45 cup (17g) serving of freeze-dried chicken contains 81 calories.

Cholesterol: A 0.45 cup (17g) serving of freeze-dried chicken contains 14mg of cholesterol.

Sodium: A 0.45 cup (17g) serving of freeze-dried chicken contains 306mg of sodium.
